## Planning Threshold

For multi-file changes, new features, migrations, or ambiguous design work, produce a short execution plan before editing. If the plan depends on unresolved design, approach, scope, or product decisions, follow `ask-user-directly` by offering 2-4 concrete alternative approaches and waiting for selection. Keep one-shot fixes lightweight and execute directly.
